The Founder

Learn Enough to Be Dangerous was founded in 2015 by Michael Hartl, Lee Donahoe, and Nick Merwin. We believe that the kind of technical sophistication taught by the Learn Enough tutorials can benefit at least a billion people, and probably more.

Michael Hartl

    • Known for his dazzling charm, rapier wit, and unrivaled humility, Michael is the creator of the Ruby on Rails Tutorial and principal author of the Learn Enough to Be Dangerous introductory sequence.

    Michael is also notorious as the founder of Tau Day and author of The Tau Manifesto, but rumors that he is secretly a supervillain are slightly exaggerated.

    Lee Donahoe

    • When he is not literally swimming with sharks or hunting powder stashes on his snowboard, you can find Lee in front of his computer designing interfaces, doing front-end development, or writing some of the interface-related Learn Enough tutorials.

    Nick Merwin

    • You may have seen him shredding guitar live with Capital Cities on Jimmy Kimmel, Conan, or The Ellen Show, but rest assured Nick is a true nerd at heart. He is just as happy shredding well-specid lines of code from a tour bus as he is from his kitchen table.
